NHS

Having a healthier Christmas, can be a time of overindulgence and whilst it is important to enjoy the celebrations it is possible to eat, drink and be merry without over doing it! Research shows that with the extra calories consumed across all of the celebrations during the festive season, we can gain on average around 1kg (roughly 2lbs) It might be the last thing on everyone's mind at this time of year when so much focus is on family get-togethers and feasting but the excess calories we eat and drink at Christmas tend to stay with us, and it's much easier to prevent weight gain than to get rid of the excess weight afterwards. The British Nutrition Foundation have put together a range of resources on how to have a healthier and enjoyable Christmas and New Year.
